Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and our desktop audio software (the "Product"), including early access and beta versions.

1. Who We Are

We are the developer of the Product ("we", "us", or "our").

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, you can contact us at:

Email: support@peachsounds.com

2. What This Policy Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Our website and landing pages
  • Our early-access and beta signup process
  • Our desktop application and/or audio plugin

It does not apply to third-party websites or services that we do not control.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you sign up for early access or beta testing, we may collect:

  • Email address (to send download links and beta access)
  • Profession or role (if you choose to provide it)

We only use this information for providing access to the Product and communicating about the beta.

3.2 Website Analytics

If you give consent (where required by law), we collect limited analytics data on our website to understand how it is used and to improve it.

This may include:

  • Pages visited
  • Button clicks and form interactions
  • Referral source (e.g. campaign or link)
  • General location (country-level)
  • Device and browser type

We use PostHog as our analytics provider.

We do not use analytics data to identify you personally on the website.

3.3 Application / Plugin Usage Data

When you use the Product, we may collect limited usage data to improve functionality and stability, such as:

  • App or plugin version
  • Operating system
  • Error or crash events
  • Feature usage events (for example, when an audio export is completed)

This data is used only to improve the Product and understand how it is used during beta testing.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies or local storage for:

  • Remembering your privacy preferences
  • Analytics (only after consent, where required)

If you are in a region that requires consent (such as the EU), analytics are not activated until you accept cookies.

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser storage or revisiting the consent settings.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide early access and beta downloads
  • Communicate important information about the Product
  • Improve the website and Product
  • Fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues

We do not sell your personal data.

6. Legal Basis for Processing (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent – for website analytics and optional information
  • Contract / request – to provide access you requested (beta access, downloads)
  • Legitimate interest – to improve and secure the Product

7. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with trusted service providers who help us operate the Product, such as:

  • Analytics providers (e.g. PostHog)
  • Email delivery providers

These providers process data only on our instructions and are required to protect it.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to:

  • Run the beta program
  • Improve the Product
  • Meet legal or operational requirements

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 10).

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards to protect your data.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at:

Email: support@peachsounds.com

11. Children's Privacy

The Product is not intended for children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

13. Contact
